Available Commands:

  • "Play" - Play a song on YouTube
  • "Capture" - Captures an image from the webcam
  • "Time" - Tells the current time
  • "Date" - Tells today's date
  • "Joke" - Tells a joke
  • "Search" - Search in Google
  • "Weather" - Shows current weather
  • "Fun fact" - Fetches a random fun fact
  • "News" - Shows the latest news headlines
